About Our College

The Vindhyachal Shikshan Sanstha

Was established in the year 1996 with vision to provide education to youth by Late.Keshavraoji Padul.

VSS college was established in 2007 with an aim to provide educationto all the category of students in the society.Our College has been designed by keeping in mind the requirement of today`s 21st Century youth. We have all such facilities, which are to be given for these students, such as well furnished lab,library with Books on subject and off subject, exprienced staff that takes care of students.By Keeping in mind the requirement of computer professionals,we are coming up with new facilities every year.


Our college provides quality education in the field of Science, Information Technology & Management Science with the vision to implement the New Education Policy designed by UGC Our objectives is to train our youth as world class knowladge workers of tomorrow and to serve society,by enriching themselves,their families and the nation at large.
